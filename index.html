Upholstery Cleaning



Our sofa cleaning process is similar to that used in our carpet cleaning procedure. A specialised hand tool is used to clean across the fabric instead of the solution being fired directly into it. The micro-splitting cleaning process will extract soil particles from the fibres, without the use of detergents or enzymes. This will ensure optimum cleaning results and prevents possible re-soiling.
